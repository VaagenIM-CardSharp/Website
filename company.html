Who we are

CardSharp is a team of designers and engineers focused on building fast, secure and elegant websites. We believe that premium quality is a combination of details — clear design systems, accessible interfaces, predictable code and reliable hosting.

Our clients choose us when deadlines are tight and quality cannot slip. We optimize Core Web Vitals, ship readable, tested code and provide post-launch care with monitoring, backups and analytics.

How we work

We start with a discovery call and a short audit of your goals, users and competition. Then we propose a scope, timeline and budget. We deliver in sprints with transparent weekly updates and demos. We value smart constraints, straightforward communication and long-term partnerships.

Our stack includes Next.js/React, Headless CMS (Sanity, Strapi), Cloudflare/Vercel, Node and Postgres. We integrate payment, authentication, analytics and marketing tools — without vendor lock-in.
